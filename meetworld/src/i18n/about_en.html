Game Description

Dimension Meetup runs on the blockchain and is a blockchain integrated platform that integrates blockchain games, blockchain activities, blockchain ticketing and user-generated content.The Dimension Meetup code is fully open source, developed for community developers, and motivated by the notarization economy.

Dimension Meetup's existing Earth module combines gameplay and openness. The future development direction is determined by community consensus.

In the Earth module, we divide the land by level, such as the earth, continents, countries, cities, landmarks, etc.These game assets are NFT(Non-Fungible Token), unique and chain-ready.Dimension Meetup will combine different levels of land to achieve different ways of playing.

In the map module, users can upload the landmarks autonomously. The landmarks are recorded on the blockchain and become user assets. The coinju provides a rich gameplay, giving users the greatest freedom to create space. Users can buy and sell landmarks, define the attributes of landmarks, hold events on landmarks, and earn income.

3、Land Trading Game

3.1 【Earth module】National trading

In order to prevent users from uploading spam, we are now leaving the landmark review to the core players of the coinju community, which will be fully open to the community in the future.

We have opened two rounds of national trading games, and we are about to update our smart contracts. We will launch the third round of internal testing on December 14, 2018. The gameplay is as follows:

The third round of testing opened 249 countries, the starting price of Token in each country was 0.1 EON, and each purchase made the country's price increase by 35%.The amount of the next purchase will be returned to the previous player's principal and the value added will be distributed as follows:

  • 60% previous player
  • 27% bonuspool
  • 3% recommender (if not, enter the bonuspool)
  • 10% jackpot

3.2 Bonuspool instructions

Mortgage EON at the Dimension Meetup "Token -> Bonuspool " , you can get the dividend of the current project income in real time. The dividend will be distributed according to the proportion of mortgage EON

3.3 Countdown instructions

The third round of testing increased the countdown function, from 30 days to 0. Every EON flow into the smart contract for the purchase of land, the countdown increased by 1 minute. The countdown was 0 when the last purchased player won the total prize pool.

4、About EON

The token for Dimension Meetup is “EON”. The holder can share the profit of the Dimension Meetup and participate in the decentralized autonomy of the platform through token mortgage.In addition, EON is also an application token, players can use EON to purchase game assets. How to get EON

  • Buy on Dimension Meetup official website
  • Mortgaging EON at Dimension Meetup can generate EON proportionally during the game；Selling EON directly to the smart contract at Dimension Meetup will be charged 5% commission；EON transfer between users is free
